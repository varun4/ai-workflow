# Half-Day Workshop

## Audience

Mixed group of developers and knowledge workers.

## Agenda

1. Introduction and framing
2. The workflow operating system
3. Specification precision and scope decomposition
4. Context architecture and evaluation
5. Security and approval boundaries
6. Hands-on exercise by audience track
7. Debrief and next steps

## Workshop Output

By the end of the session, each participant should leave with a draft
workflow spec, an `AGENTS.md` pattern, a `PROGRESS.md` pattern, and one
evaluation checklist they can apply immediately.
