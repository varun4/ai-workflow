# Specs

Specs translate a goal into executable work.

## A Good Spec Includes

- Objective
- Scope
- Constraints
- Inputs and dependencies
- Success criteria
- Validation method
- Explicit non-goals when necessary

## Why Specs Matter

- They reduce hidden assumptions
- They clarify what is out of scope
- They make evaluation possible

## Teaching Points

- Most agent failures start as spec failures
- Precision matters more than length
- The best specs are specific enough to execute and short enough to review
