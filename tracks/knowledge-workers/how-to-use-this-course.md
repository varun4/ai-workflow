# Knowledge Worker Track Guide

Use the shared docs as the main framework, then translate each concept
into research, writing, operations, and synthesis workflows.

## Focus Areas

- research briefs
- meeting outputs
- writing workflows
- review and approval loops
- handling sensitive information safely

## Most Relevant Templates

- `templates/shared/AGENTS.example.md`
- `templates/shared/PROGRESS.example.md`
- `templates/shared/SPEC.example.md`

## Capstone Direction

Design an agent-assisted workflow for a recurring knowledge task such as
research synthesis or decision-support preparation.
