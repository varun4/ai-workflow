# Skill: Evidence Quality Review

## Purpose

Provide a repeatable quality check for recommendation briefs.

## Inputs

- Brief draft
- Source list
- Scoring table

## Outputs

- Review findings
- Required revisions
- Approval recommendation

## Constraints

- Do not add new claims without cited source support.
- Flag unsupported statements as blockers.

## Checklist

- Every major claim has a citation.
- Comparison criteria are applied consistently.
- Assumptions are labeled clearly.
- Recommendation logic follows scored evidence.
- Confidential information is handled per policy.

## Verification

- Confirm all checklist items in `EVAL_CHECKLIST.md` are complete.
- Require stakeholder approval when unresolved gaps remain.
